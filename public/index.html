Types of Tanks:

Light Tank: 
A fast and agile tank with a quick-firing single-barrel gun.

Base Speed: 8
Base Cooldown (time between shots): 250ms
Base Damage: 5 (each bullet does 5 damage)
Medium Tank: 
A well-balanced tank with a double-barrel gun.

Base Speed: 4
Base Cooldown: 500ms
Base Damage: 10 (each bullet does 5 damage, 2 bullets per shot)
Heavy Tank: 
A slow, heavily-armored tank with a powerful four-barrel gun.

Base Speed: 2
Base Cooldown: 800ms
Base Damage: 20 (each bullet does 5 damage, 4 bullets per shot)
Super-Heavy Tank: 
An extremely slow tank with a devastating six-barrel gun.

Base Speed: 0.5
Base Cooldown: 1200ms
Base Damage: 30 (each bullet does 5 damage, 6 bullets per shot)

As you level up in the game, your tank's capabilities will improve. Leveling up affects the following attributes:

Damage: Each level adds 1 additional bullet to your tank's shot, increasing the total damage dealt per shot. Speed: Your tank's speed increases with each level, allowing you to move more quickly around the battlefield. Turn Speed: The rotation speed of your tank increases with each level, enabling faster response to enemy threats.
